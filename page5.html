Superstore Sales Data Analysis (Tableau)

Analyzed Superstore sales data in Tableau and designed a comprehensive Figma dashboard template for user-friendly, insightful data representation and decision-making support

  • Github Link

Project Overview :

I embarked on a data-driven journey to transform raw sales data from a Superstore into actionable insights. Leveraging the power of Tableau, I designed an interactive and visually engaging dashboard that provides a comprehensive view of the store's sales performance. To complement this, I created a versatile template for the dashboard's design using Figma.



Project Details :

Data Analysis with Tableau :


Total Customers: Count of unique individuals who have made purchases, providing an understanding of customer volume.

Average Sales per Customer: The average amount spent by each customer, indicating their typical spending behavior.

Customer Distribution by Sales: Visualization showing how customers are distributed across different spending ranges, helping segment your customer base.

Top 10 Customers: Identification of the ten customers with the highest total purchases, highlighting the most significant contributors to sales.

Sales per Customer by Category: Average spending per customer categorized by product type, revealing which categories are preferred by customers.

Regional Sales: Breakdown of sales by geographic regions (West, East, Central, South), helping you identify regional sales trends.

Sales by States: Total sales revenue generated in each state, assisting in identifying high-performing regions.

Total Sales: The sum of all sales revenue, providing a comprehensive view of overall revenue.

Total Profit: The total profit earned, indicating the financial success of the Superstore.

Profit Margin: Profit as a percentage of sales, revealing how efficiently the Superstore operates.

Total Quantity: The total number of products sold, helping in inventory management and tracking sales volume.

Sales and Profit by States: Combined view of both sales and profit data, allowing you to assess financial performance at the state level.

Sales and Profit by Sub-Category: Breakdown of sales and profit by specific product sub-categories, providing insights into product performance.
